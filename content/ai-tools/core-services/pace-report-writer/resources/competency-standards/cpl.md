# Competency Standards — Corporal / Able Seaman (Cpl/AB)

> **2026 Competency Framework Reference**
> Use this document when scoring Behavioural Indicators (BIs) in PaCE for a Cpl/AB.
> **Score 3 = Effective = the expected standard.** Most members should score 3 on most BIs.

---

## Rating Scale Quick Reference

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Ineffective | Does not demonstrate this behaviour. Requires immediate intervention or remediation. |
| 2 | Partially Effective | Demonstrates inconsistently. Needs coaching and periodic reminders. |
| 3 | Effective | Consistently meets the BI as written. This is the standard — not exceptional. |
| 4 | Above Effective | Consistently meets BI and demonstrates initiative or depth clearly beyond minimum. Occasionally shows MCpl-level behaviours. |
| 5 | Extremely Effective | Routinely demonstrates MCpl-level behaviours. Rare — reserve for truly outstanding performance. |

| Action Orientation and Initiative | Taking Action | Delays action or takes inappropriate actions; timing and judgment are frequently off. | Takes action but timing is inconsistent; occasionally acts without thinking through consequences. | Consistently takes appropriate and timely action when required. | Acts with sound judgment under time pressure; anticipates when action will be needed. | Keeps CoC informed of risks associated with a COA — MCpl-level situational awareness. |
