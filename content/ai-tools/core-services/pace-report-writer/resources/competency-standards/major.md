# Competency Standards — Major / Lieutenant-Commander (Major/LCdr)

> **2026 Competency Framework Reference**
> Use this document when scoring Behavioural Indicators (BIs) in PaCE for a Major/LCdr.
> **Score 3 = Effective = the expected standard.**
> For next-rank indicators, refer to [lcol.md](lcol.md).

---

## Rating Scale Quick Reference

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Ineffective | Does not demonstrate this behaviour. Requires immediate intervention. |
| 2 | Partially Effective | Demonstrates inconsistently. Needs coaching and follow-up. |
| 3 | Effective | Consistently meets the BI as written. The expected standard. |
| 4 | Above Effective | Consistently meets BI; occasionally shows LCol-level behaviours. |
| 5 | Extremely Effective | Routinely demonstrates LCol-level behaviours. Reserve for outstanding performance. |
