# Competency Standards — Major-General / Rear Admiral (MGen/RAdm)

> **2026 Competency Framework Reference**
> Use this document when scoring Behavioural Indicators (BIs) in PaCE for a MGen/RAdm.
> **Score 3 = Effective = the expected standard.**
> Score 5 reflects the highest institutional standard. MGen is the senior strategic rank in this framework — Score 5 describes outstanding performance at this level.

---

## Rating Scale Quick Reference

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Ineffective | Does not demonstrate this behaviour. Requires immediate intervention. |
| 2 | Partially Effective | Demonstrates inconsistently. Needs coaching and follow-up. |
| 3 | Effective | Consistently meets the BI as written. The expected standard. |
| 4 | Above Effective | Consistently meets BI; demonstrates with exceptional depth or breadth. |
| 5 | Extremely Effective | Rare, outstanding performance that redefines the institutional standard. Reserve for truly exceptional performance. |
