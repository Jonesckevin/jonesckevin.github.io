# Competency Standards — Captain / Lieutenant (Navy) (Capt/Lt(N))

> **2026 Competency Framework Reference**
> Use this document when scoring Behavioural Indicators (BIs) in PaCE for a Capt/Lt(N).
> **Score 3 = Effective = the expected standard.**
> For next-rank indicators, refer to [major.md](major.md).

---

## Rating Scale Quick Reference

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Ineffective | Does not demonstrate this behaviour. Requires immediate intervention. |
| 2 | Partially Effective | Demonstrates inconsistently. Needs coaching and follow-up. |
| 3 | Effective | Consistently meets the BI as written. The expected standard. |
| 4 | Above Effective | Consistently meets BI; occasionally shows Major-level behaviours. |
| 5 | Extremely Effective | Routinely demonstrates Major-level behaviours. Reserve for outstanding performance. |

| Technical / Professional Proficiency | Demonstrating Technical Knowledge | Significant gaps in tactical-level occupational knowledge, skills, or abilities. | Knowledge is generally adequate but mastery at the tactical level is not consistently demonstrated. | Consistently demonstrates tactical-level knowledge, skills, abilities, qualifications, and experience. | Tactical expertise is recognized; contributes knowledge beyond own role when needed. | Demonstrates expert knowledge, skills, and abilities at the operational level — Major-level knowledge. |
