# Supervisor Checklist — Quarterly Feedback Note Debrief

> Use this checklist for Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q4 feedback-note debriefs.
> Keep entries evidence-based and aligned with observed behaviours.
> Use this as a consistent sequence so members at all ranks receive the same debrief structure.

---

## 1) Quarter Preparation (Before Debrief)

- [ ] Review prior FNs, MAP, and pending actions
- [ ] Gather examples tied to behaviour and impact
- [ ] Confirm quarter deadline and schedule 20–45 min debrief
- [ ] Prepare simple agenda in order: outcomes, gaps, MAP status, next priorities
- [ ] Confirm any unresolved items from last quarter

---

## 2) During the Quarter (Evidence Capture)

- [ ] Conduct ad-hoc feedback session when required (member or supervisor initiated)
- [ ] Write event FN(s) with specific behaviour/evidence
- [ ] Set FN to Ready and obtain member acknowledgement
- [ ] Log any risks or welfare/admin issues requiring CoC support

---

## 3) End-of-Quarter Summary FN (Required)

- [ ] Write quarter summary FN:
- [ ] Q1 summary FN (NLT 1st week Jul)
- [ ] Q2 summary FN (NLT 1st week Oct)
- [ ] Q3 summary FN (NLT mid-Dec)
- [ ] Q4 end-year summary FN (NLT mid-Mar)
- [ ] Debrief summary FN with member
- [ ] Ensure member acknowledges summary FN

---

## 4) Conduct Debrief (Simple Ordered Script)

### A) Open Meeting

- [ ] Confirm purpose and quarter period covered
- [ ] Confirm expected flow: review -> questions -> actions -> acknowledgment

### B) Review Results in Order

- [ ] What went well (specific evidence)
- [ ] What needs improvement (specific and actionable)
- [ ] MAP status (on track / adjust / complete)
- [ ] Next quarter expectations and priorities

### C) Confirm Understanding

- [ ] Ask member to summarize key takeaways in their own words
- [ ] Address questions in sequence (evidence -> expectations -> support)
- [ ] Record significant member comments

### D) Close Debrief

- [ ] Confirm immediate action items (who, what, due date)
- [ ] Confirm FN acknowledgment process before ending session

---

## 5) Quarter-Specific Checks

- [ ] Q2/Q3: for PO2/Sgt and above, confirm Opt-Out intent and remind **15 Jan** deadline (DND 4638)
- [ ] Q4: ensure full-year FN record is complete for PAR drafting
- [ ] If APS posting: losing unit writes summary FN and debriefs before departure

---

## Quarterly Timing Reference

| Quarter | Required Summary FN | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Q1 Summary FN | NLT 1st week of Jul |
| Q2 | Q2 Summary FN | NLT 1st week of Oct |
| Q3 | Q3 Summary FN | NLT mid-Dec |
| Q4 | Q4 End-Year Summary FN | NLT mid-Mar |

> **Note:** The exact quarter timing may vary by unit/command. Confirm with your CoC.

---

## 6) What to Log and Where to Log It

- [ ] **Monitor MASS / PaCE record:** FN status, acknowledgment status, summary FN completion
- [ ] **Unit section tracker:** debrief date/time, attendees, quarter completion status
- [ ] **Supervisor continuity notes:** coaching focus, emerging trends, next-quarter priorities
- [ ] **CoC / PaCE Manager notification record (if required):** issue raised, notification date/time, follow-up direction

---

## 7) After Debrief

- [ ] Confirm FN status is complete and acknowledged
- [ ] Record follow-up actions and due dates
- [ ] Calendar next debrief
- [ ] Escalate issues requiring CoC / Admin / welfare support

---

## 8) Topic Order (Quick Reference)

1. Quarter scope and purpose
2. Evidence-backed strengths
3. Improvement priorities
4. MAP progress and adjustments
5. Next-quarter expectations
6. Actions, suspense, and FN acknowledgment
