# Supervisor Checklist — Quarterly Feedback Note Debrief

> Use this checklist for Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q4 feedback-note debriefs.
> Keep entries evidence-based and aligned with observed behaviours.

---

## 1) Pre-Meeting

- [ ] Review prior FNs, MAP, and pending actions
- [ ] Gather examples tied to behaviour and impact
- [ ] Confirm quarter deadline and schedule 20–45 min debrief

---

## 2) During the Quarter (as needed)

- [ ] Conduct ad-hoc feedback session when required (member or supervisor initiated)
- [ ] Write event FN(s) with specific behaviour/evidence
- [ ] Set FN to Ready and obtain member acknowledgement

---

## 3) End-of-Quarter Summary FN

- [ ] Write quarter summary FN:
  - [ ] Q1 summary FN (NLT 1st week Jul)
  - [ ] Q2 summary FN (NLT 1st week Oct)
  - [ ] Q3 summary FN (NLT mid-Dec)
  - [ ] Q4 end-year summary FN (NLT mid-Mar)
- [ ] Debrief summary FN with member
- [ ] Ensure member acknowledges summary FN

---

## 4) Debrief Content (Keep It Simple)

- [ ] What went well (specific evidence)
- [ ] What needs improvement (specific and actionable)
- [ ] MAP status (on track / adjust / complete)
- [ ] Next quarter expectations and priorities

---

## 5) Quarter-Specific Checks

- [ ] Q2/Q3: confirm Opt-Out intent and remind **15 Jan** deadline (DND 4638)
- [ ] Q4: ensure full-year FN record is complete for PAR drafting
- [ ] If APS posting: losing unit writes summary FN and debriefs before departure

---

## Quarterly Timing Reference

| Quarter | Required Summary FN | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Q1 Summary FN | NLT 1st week of Jul |
| Q2 | Q2 Summary FN | NLT 1st week of Oct |
| Q3 | Q3 Summary FN | NLT mid-Dec |
| Q4 | Q4 End-Year Summary FN | NLT mid-Mar |

> **Note:** The exact quarter timing may vary by unit/command. Confirm with your CoC.

---

## After Debrief

- [ ] Confirm FN status is complete and acknowledged
- [ ] Record follow-up actions and due dates
- [ ] Calendar next debrief
- [ ] Escalate issues requiring CoC / Admin / welfare support

---
