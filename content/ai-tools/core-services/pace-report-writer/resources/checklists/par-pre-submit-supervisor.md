# Supervisor Checklist — PAR Pre-Submit Quality Check

> Use this checklist before sending a PAR to IR/RO.
> Goal: catch common issues early and reduce PAR returns.

---

## 1) Readiness Gate (Do Not Submit Until Complete)

- [ ] Correct PAR roles are assigned (Author, IR if required, RO, SA)
- [ ] Reporting period is correct
- [ ] Member rank, position context, and employment details are correct
- [ ] All required sections are complete (no placeholders or blank mandatory fields)
- [ ] Supporting FNs are complete and acknowledged where required

---

## 2) Evidence-to-Score Check (Return Prevention Core)

- [ ] Each key score has at least one specific evidence point
- [ ] Evidence references actual observed behaviour, not assumptions
- [ ] Evidence includes impact (on team, task, mission, or unit)
- [ ] Score pattern is coherent (no unexplained extremes)
- [ ] A score of 3 is treated as expected standard unless evidence supports higher

---

## 3) Narrative Quality Check (Author and RO)

- [ ] Writing is clear, specific, and professional
- [ ] Statements are rank-appropriate and role-appropriate
- [ ] No contradictions between score, evidence, and comments
- [ ] Development areas include actionable direction
- [ ] Tone is balanced (strengths + improvement priorities)

---

## 4) Common Return Triggers to Eliminate

- [ ] Vague statements without evidence (for example: "excellent member" with no support)
- [ ] Inflated language not matched by score or FN evidence
- [ ] Copy-paste text that does not match this member's year
- [ ] Missing context for low or high outlier scores
- [ ] Administrative mismatch (dates, role chain, status)

### Author Note Example (Use as Pattern)

> "Member consistently delivered assigned tasks to standard across the reporting year. In Q2 and Q3, they led section-level coordination for time-sensitive deliverables and maintained output quality under deadline pressure. Feedback Notes dated 15 Jul, 03 Oct, and 12 Dec support strong performance in planning, communication, and accountability. Development focus remains improving delegation under high workload so team capacity is better distributed."

---

## 6) RO Note Checklist

- [ ] RO note validates or adjusts author assessment with rationale
- [ ] Highlights comparative context where appropriate
- [ ] Confirms development direction and expectations for next cycle
- [ ] Stays evidence-based and aligned with PAR content

### RO Note Example (Use as Pattern)

> "Reviewing Officer concurs with the author assessment. Evidence in the FN record supports consistent performance at current rank, with notable strength in execution reliability and coordination during high-tempo periods. I concur with the identified development priority of delegation and expect focused progress in next-cycle task distribution, coaching, and supervisory oversight."

---

## 7) Final Pre-Submit Sequence

1. Confirm role chain and admin accuracy
2. Re-check score-to-evidence alignment
3. Finalize Author Note
4. If IR applies, confirm package is complete for IR review
5. Confirm RO Note intent and supporting rationale readiness
6. Submit PAR forward only when all boxes are complete

---

## 8) What to Log and Where

- [ ] **Monitor MASS / PaCE record:** PAR status changes and workflow handoff state
- [ ] **Unit section tracker:** submission date/time, who reviewed, return status if any
- [ ] **Supervisor continuity notes:** known risk items and coaching priorities for follow-up
- [ ] **CoC / PaCE Manager notification record (if needed):** blockers, decisions, and direction received
