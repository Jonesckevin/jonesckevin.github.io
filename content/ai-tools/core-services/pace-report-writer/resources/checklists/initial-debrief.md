# Supervisor Checklist — Initial PaCE Debrief

> Use this checklist when conducting the **Initial Debrief** with a subordinate at the start of the PaCE cycle.
> This meeting establishes the baseline for the full cycle.
> Use this as a simple sequence so every member receives the same briefing order.

---

## 1) Pre-Meeting Readiness (Supervisor)

- [ ] Confirm you are assigned as PaCE Supervisor in Monitor MASS
- [ ] Review current JD status (draft/finalized) and any prior cycle context
- [ ] Review member rank and applicable competency standards
- [ ] Confirm meeting time and private location (30–60 min)
- [ ] Confirm you can show the member their JD and MAP during the session
- [ ] Prepare a short agenda to share at the start

---

## 2) Prepare the Debrief Package (Before Meeting)

- [ ] Provide member meeting details in advance (time, location, expected duration)
- [ ] Share JD draft, if available, so the member can preview duties
- [ ] Prepare sequence notes for the meeting:
- [ ] JD review and corrections
- [ ] MAP review and alignment
- [ ] Opt-Out policy (if applicable)
- [ ] Initial mandatory FNs and acknowledgment steps

---

## 3) Conduct Initial Debrief (Ordered Script)

### A) Open Meeting

- [ ] Confirm purpose: start-of-cycle baseline, expectations, and documentation
- [ ] Confirm scope: what will be reviewed and finalized today
- [ ] Confirm expected flow: JD -> MAP -> Opt-Out policy -> initial FNs -> close-out

### B) Joint JD and MAP Review

- [ ] Review JD with member and confirm duties are accurate
- [ ] If JD needs correction, update before finalizing
- [ ] Finalize JD (member can un-finalize within 7 days without PaCE Manager)
- [ ] Review/update MAP (immediate, medium, long-term goals)
- [ ] Acknowledge MAP (acknowledgement is not commitment to all aspirations)

### C) Confirm Policy Items

- [ ] Explain Opt-Out policy clearly:
- [ ] Only PO2/Sgt and above may opt out via DND 4638
- [ ] Cpl/S1 and MCpl/MS are included in potential processing automatically
- [ ] Deadline remains 15 January for eligible members

---

## 4) Initial Mandatory Feedback Notes (NLT 30 Apr)

- [ ] FN 1 complete: Initial review of JD and MAP
- [ ] FN 2 complete: Secondary duties
- [ ] FN 3 complete: Inclusive behaviours
- [ ] Debrief all 3 FNs in sequence and ensure member acknowledges each FN
- [ ] If member does not acknowledge, document reason and follow CoC/PaCE Manager process

---

## 5) What to Log and Where to Log It

- [ ] **Monitor MASS / PaCE record:** JD and MAP status, FN status, member acknowledgments
- [ ] **Unit section tracker:** debrief date/time, attendees, completion status, suspense items
- [ ] **Supervisor continuity notes:** member goals, concern areas, coaching priorities for Q1
- [ ] **CoC / PaCE Manager notification record (if required):** issue raised, who was notified, date/time

---

## 6) Close-Out and Next Step

- [ ] Confirm member can access their PaCE file
- [ ] Confirm member received required documents/information before ending session
- [ ] Record debrief date and key actions
- [ ] Set next checkpoint for Q1 review (NLT 1st week of July)

---

## 7) Topic Order (Quick Reference)

1. Purpose and scope
2. JD review and corrections
3. MAP goals and alignment
4. Opt-Out policy (if eligible)
5. Initial mandatory FNs and acknowledgment
6. Follow-up actions and suspense dates
