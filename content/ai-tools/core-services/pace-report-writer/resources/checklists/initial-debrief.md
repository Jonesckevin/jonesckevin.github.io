# Supervisor Checklist — Initial PaCE Debrief

> Use this checklist when conducting the **Initial Debrief** with a subordinate at the start of the PaCE cycle.
> This meeting establishes the baseline for the full cycle.

---

## 1) Pre-Meeting (Supervisor)

- [ ] Confirm you are assigned as PaCE Supervisor in Monitor MASS
- [ ] Review current JD status (draft/finalized) and any prior cycle context
- [ ] Review member rank and applicable competency standards
- [ ] Confirm meeting time and private location (30–60 min)

---

## 2) Joint JD and MAP Review

- [ ] Review JD with member and confirm duties are accurate
- [ ] If JD needs correction, update before finalizing
- [ ] Finalize JD (member can un-finalize within 7 days without PaCE Manager)
- [ ] Review/update MAP (immediate, medium, long-term goals)
- [ ] Acknowledge MAP (acknowledgement is not commitment to all aspirations)

---

## 3) Opt-Out / Opt-In (If Applicable)

- [ ] Confirm whether member is considering Opt-Out
- [ ] Explain DND 4638 process and **15 Jan deadline**
- [ ] If opting out, notify/coordinate with PaCE Manager for processing

---

## 4) Initial Mandatory Feedback Notes (NLT 30 Apr)

- [ ] FN 1 complete: Initial review of JD and MAP
- [ ] FN 2 complete: Secondary duties
- [ ] FN 3 complete: Inclusive behaviours
- [ ] Conduct debrief and ensure member acknowledges all 3 FNs

---

## 5) Close-Out and Next Step

- [ ] Confirm member can access their PaCE file
- [ ] Record debrief date and key actions
- [ ] Set next checkpoint for Q1 review (NLT 1st week of July)
